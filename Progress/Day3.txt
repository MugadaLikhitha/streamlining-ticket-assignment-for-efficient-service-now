###Day 3 – Flow Designer Automation

Objective:
Automate issue assignment using Flow Designer to route requests to the correct group based on the issue type.

Work Completed:

Flow 1: Regarding Certificate

Condition: Issue = Regarding Certificates.

Flow 2: Regarding Platform
Trigger: Create/Update Record on Operations Related table.
Conditions:
Issue = Unable to login to platform

Action: Update Record → Assigned to Group = Platform.
